Our Mission

Bringing data-driven decisions to every field.

Agriculture still depends heavily on guesswork. Choosing the wrong crop for a piece of land wastes water, fertilizer, time and money. Our system replaces that guesswork with a trained machine learning model that reads your soil and weather conditions and instantly recommends the crop that will thrive best.

Built as a final-year software engineering project, it is designed to be lightweight, accessible from any browser, and useful for farmers, agriculture students and research institutions alike.

  • Increase yield with the right crop choice
  • Reduce fertilizer and water waste
  • Free, browser-based, mobile friendly

Project Objectives

What this project sets out to achieve

  • 01
    Accuracy. Build a model that classifies crops with high real-world accuracy (~99.5%).
  • 02
    Accessibility. Deliver it via a clean web app any farmer or student can use.
  • 03
    Education. Demonstrate a complete ML lifecycle for academic and portfolio use.
  • 04
    Scalability. Structure the codebase so it can grow to include auth, history and analytics.
